Cloudiness vs. Latitude


This plot shows the percentage of cloud cover at each location.

Groupings can be seen along specific percentage lines: 0%, 20%, 40%, 75%, 90% and 100%. This may be due to the way some of the data is reported/collected - as “mostly cloudy” or “partly cloudy”.
